pizza dough

Description

This is a recipe for making pizza dough for chicago style pizza

Ingredients

  • 3 cups AP flour
  • 0.5 cups cornmeal
  • 2 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp yeast
  • 1 cup water
  • 0.5 cup butter
  1. Combine flour, cornmeal, salt, sugar, an yeast together in a bowl
  2. Use a stand mixer or your hands to mix the ingredients together into a dough
  3. Once the dough has come togehter, add the water and half the butter butter
  4. Mix until supple and not too hard
  5. Grease a bowl and allow the dough to rise for an hour, covered
